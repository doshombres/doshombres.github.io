About Us

Dos Hombres are an acoustic duo based in Sydney.

We are Christopher Horn and Greg Lindsay. Two experienced musicians who will take the time to discuss and understand your needs and will work with you to get the right sound for your event.

The feeling of having a live performance at your function can’t be imitated. Whether you’re looking for music to be a central theme of your celebration or want something that stays in the background and complements the tone of your event, we can tailor our performance to your needs.
